Here are my top three picks of Sweatshirts currently sold by BR. All of these shirts will definitely be a good fit for you to when going out to a rave or just casually hangin out with your friends.

OG Undyed HoodieEris Drew x Boiler Room Face Behind My Eyes HoodieEris Drew x Boiler Room Luv Dancing Hoodie

The first one is the OG Undyed Hoodie, which takes inspiration from BR raw, uncut approach. I like this better compared to the original one because of its lighter color, making it much more flexible to be used for different occasions. Other than that, the color is also made without dyeing, so the color will not be ruined when you accidently wash it off with other colored clothes.

The next one is the Eris Drew x BR Face Behind My Eyes Hoodie, which is a product from the collab between BR and Eris drew. The design mimics the OG undyed hoodie, but with touch of Eris Drew logo in it. Each of these Garment boasts original artwork of artist Alfie Kungu, and I beg the loose fits will be as comfortable for a dance floor as it would be for a DJ Battle.

The last one would be Eris Drew X BR Luv Dancing Hoodie. This hoodie is a bit different from the previous one as it has a black base color and also moren psychadelic artwork visible on all sides of the hoodie. This is my personal favorite just because of the abundance of the artwork in it, and I would recommend you to use this whenever you go to a Rave, or just when you are chillin with your friends.
