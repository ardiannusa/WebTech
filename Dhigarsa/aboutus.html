About Us

Dhigarsa is an investment platform specific for sustainable aquaculture farm production with low risks, low environmental footprint, and high Return Of Investment (ROI).


Vision and Mission

Vision

To become a leading company that contributes to the advancement of the aquaculture industry in Indonesia.

Mission

We will collaborate with various investors and stakeholders to build and develop aquaculture businesses in multiple regions in Indonesia.

Our Business

Our current main business is Vannamei shrimp farming, but we plan to expand to brackish water fish farming (Milkfish) and freshwater fish farming (catfish, tilapia). Other than that, we will also expand to domestic and international fish and shrimp trading in the future.

Shrimp Farming

The shrimp that we cultivate in our farms are whiteleg shrimp (Litopenaeus Vannamei). These species of shrimp make up 75% of Indonesia's total shrimp export, which is valued at US$ 1.7 Bn or close to 175,000 tonnes.

Fish Farming

The fishes that we cultivate in our farms are milkfish (Chanos Chanos). The milkfish is highly valued as a food fish in Indonesia, and it is one of the most important fish species being farmed in the country. The production value of milkfish amounted to 822,37 metric tonnes in 2019.

Aquaculture Trading

Fishes or shrimps that are sold from our trading business are sourced from different aquaculture farms in Java and Sumatera. The main buyers for our trading business will be domestic restaurants, domestic food processing companies, and foreign importers from the U.S.

Our Milestones

2021: Establishment & 1st Cycle

On 2021, PT Dhigarsa Agrapana Samudera or also known as 'Dhigarsa' was founded as a limited company that focuses on aquaculture cultivation by five partners - Kevin Demelza (CEO), Emirio Syarfuan (CFO), Ardian Sukamdani (President Commissioner), Daffa Ganis (Commissioner), and Rizky Adhi (Commissioner) in Jakarta. Afterwards, the company finalized the construction of the its first shrimp farm project in Sukaasih Village West Java and succesfully execute the first cylce of operations.

2022: Continuation of Operations & 2nd Project

On 2022, Dhigarsa plans continue the 2nd and 3rd cycle of operations of its shrimp farm in Sukaasih village. At the same time, the Dhigarsa team will also focus on gathering all the necessary capital and shrimp farming resources to build and operate its second project in the coastal area of Subang.

2023: Expansion to Fish Farming & Developing Trading Business

On 2023, Dhigarsa will begin expanding to freshwater and saltwater fish farming by cultivating milkfish and unagi. These farms will be integrated to our existing shrimp farm to achieve our goal of creating a fully integrated sustainable aquaculture farm in Indonesia. On top of that Dhigarsa will also start to develop its aquaculture trading business by partnering up with various aquaculture farmers in Indonesia (starting from Java and Sumatera) and either sell their products domestically to fulfill the needs of the seafood processing industry in Indonesia or export it to foreign buyers in the US, EU, or Latin America.

2024-Onwards: Operating >10 Aquaculture Farms Across the Archipelago

From 2024 onwards, we will further expand our integrated aquaculture farming business to more than 10 locations across the archipelago, while also exponentially grow our aquaculutre trading business to more buyers domestically and internationally. With this objective in mind, we aim to become the pioneer that spearhead blue ocean revolution locally and internationally.
