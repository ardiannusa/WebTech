Innovation and Sustainability

Dhigarsa is an investment platform specific for sustainable aquaculture farm production that has low risks, low environmental footprint, and high Return Of Investment (ROI).

Our Story

Our story begins in 2020, when one of the co-founders, Kevin, visited his friend's shrimp farm in the Bangka Islands area. There, Kevin helped the procession of frying the fry, recorded the income from each partial harvest, and interacted directly with the feeders who worked in the pond. Seeing the impact and potential of his friend's aquaculture business, Kevin then invited other college relatives – Daffa, Rio, Ardian and Adhi – to learn more about the vannamei shrimp aquaculture business.

For more than 6 months, these five relatives visited various kinds of ponds in Java and Sumatra and met with experts in the field of shrimp farming to explore in detail how a pond is operated. After gaining sufficient knowledge, the co-founders finally ventured to establish Dhigarsa, and began operating its first fish pond in Subang in 2021.

Industry Prospect

According to the FAO, Aquaculture production reached 16.6 metric tonnes in 2021, which makes Indonesia the second largest producers of seafood in aquaculture farms after China. Top aquaculture products exported include shrimp, fish and seaweed. Today, Aquaculture has seen a tremendous growth in its contribution to fish supply in Indonesia, increasing from 10.6% in 1960 to 40.2% in Indonesia, in which the industry looks to surpass the output of capture fisheries by 2026 under BAU scenario. These trends makes aquaculture a promising business for investors to invest in the long-run as demand in the industry expands.

Our Value Proposition

Sustainability

All of our farm uses sustainable aquaculture farming system that utilizes green waste management system to treat our used water before displacing it to the sea.

High Survival Rate

Our farm has a high survival rate that ranges from 85% to 90%. With high survival rate, this increases the productivity of our farm, and hence its profitability.

Competitive Return of Investment (ROI)

The ROI of our farm ranges from 138% (3 yr) to 250% (5 yr) due to the state of the art farming system that mitigates disease risk among the aquaculture species that we cultivate.

Business Transparency

By utilizing JALA Tech as our financial management software, investors would be able to get a transparent view the way their investment are being used in real-time.
